What is Todewr?

Todewr is the past, present & future. It's the latest & greatest, but remembers its roots.

Todewr is SMS based text storage.

Imagine being able to store all of your simple 'strings' in one place. Now imagine being able to revisit those incredible letters and numbers at a later time. The possibilities are as boundless as your imagination.

Todewr means every scribe's dream has come true.

We've melded the convention of paper with the convenience of the internet to bring you this state of the art list-making tool. Now you can just text your thoughts or items to us and retrieve them at your leisure.

Commands

Todewr makes the follow commands available to you:

Add an item:

'<Item Name>' adds to default 'todo' list

'add <Item Name> to <ListName >' adds to specified list

Create a list:

'create new list <ListName>' adds a list with the desired name

Retrieve a list:

'get <ListName>' will return everything from the specified list

Remove an item:

'delete <Item Name> from <ListName >' removes the item from the specified list

Remove a list:

'delete <ListName>' removes the specified list
